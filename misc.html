ICPC

I am a former ICPC participant and never stopped being involved in the German ICPC community. If you have questions for example how to host a contestsite for the GCPC, feel free to reach out to me.
To get information about ICPC in Germany I recommend the following websites:

  • Winter Contest and GCPC the websites of the two annual German programming competitions and
  • CPUlm, the website of the "Verein zur Förderung des kompetitiven Programmierens", an association founded in 2020 to support competitive programming in Germany.

"Never stopped being involved" means roughly the following:

  • 2025-2026: co-organizer of NWERC at KIT, Karlsruhe
  • since 2023: co-organizer of CPUlm competitive programming days, annual event to prepare German students for the ICPC
  • since 2022: head of Jury and organizer of the annual Winter Contest and GCPC
  • since 2020: co-coach of the ICPC team of Ulm University, Germany
  • 2015-2019: participant (NWERC Bronze medal 2019, GCPC Gold medal 2018)

WG

I was a co-organizer of WG 2023 in Fribourg, Switzerland and I made and maintain the general website of the WG conference series.

Further Interests
